Design Directions for Routinelle

These mockups explore different ways Routinelle can feel calm, neutral, scientific, and easy to use. The goal is to choose a direction that supports the first routine experience without making the product feel like a store, medical diagnostic app, or influencer tool.

Evaluation Criteria

Does it feel trustworthy?
Can users scan the routine fast?
Does science feel clear, not heavy?
Does it avoid store-first pressure?
Does safety feel calm?
Would it work on mobile?

Recommended Direction

The strongest MVP direction is a hybrid of Clinical Calm and Quiz First, with selected elements from Safety First and Progress Coach. This gives Routinelle trust, speed, safety boundaries, and a reason to return after the first routine.
